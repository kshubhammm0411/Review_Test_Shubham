Faq

Q.
How Can I Edit a Heading/Paragraph/links, etc?
A.

1. Select the element to edit

2. Go to the Style Panel and on under Selector SELECT the AllTag.

3. Make your edits.

Q.
How to make Home a Layout?
A.

1. Go to Pages Panel, click on the Settings (gear) Icon.

2. On top right click on the home icon.

3. Click on Make Home Page.

Recommendations

Use correct size Images

1. Use suggest image sizes for your hero and thumbnail images.

Optimize your Images.

In order to achieve better loading time we recommend to optimize all your pictures using services likeTinypng.com

Alternatively you can use Shift + Ctrl + I to make your images responsive on Webflow.

Use SVG icons

For a high quality visual experience for your user we recommend using svg icons. You visit flaticon.es to find and customize svg icons.

Update the Notifications email address

If you want to receive the messages from your contact form you need to update the Notification email address.
